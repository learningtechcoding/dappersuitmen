Privacy Policy

At DapperSuitMen, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal details such as name, email, phone number, shipping address, and payment details to process your orders and enhance your shopping experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to process transactions, personalize your experience, provide customer support, and send relevant promotions.

3. Data Protection

Your data is secured through encryption, firewalls, and strict access controls to ensure the highest level of protection.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal data. However, we may share it with trusted third-party partners for payment processing, shipping, and marketing services.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You can access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can also opt out of marketing emails.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to improve website functionality and offer personalized recommendations. You can manage your cookie settings through your browser.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and recommend reviewing their policies.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
